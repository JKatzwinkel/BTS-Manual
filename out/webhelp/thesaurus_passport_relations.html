Relations

Relations tab gives information on the relation between the thesaurus entries. The default setting is a "partOf" relation to the parent element.

Relation

Choose between "Partof" and "is cross-reference to" relation:

  • Partof: Your current entry is a part (child) of another.
  • Is cross-reference to: This references your entry to another entry.
  • Object: Enter the Name of the related object here.
  • Search Object: Search for the object in the corpora of your current project.
  • Add Relation: Click to add more relations.
  • Remove Relation: Removes a relation from the object.
