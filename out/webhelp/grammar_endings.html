Endings

Morphological endings are to be marked by “.”, endings of word formations by “,”. Endings of personal names have to be written at the end of the full name, no matter if a name consists of compound words and/or suffixes.

Examples
Morphology: rmṯ.w(Menschen) Word Formation:mn,w(Denkmal)
Personal Name: jni̯-jt(j)=f.j
