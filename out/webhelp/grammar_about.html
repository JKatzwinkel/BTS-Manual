About

Every text is a standalone element and is at least a part (child) of a text-corpus. It can relate to another corpus or to an object directly by being attached to it or indirectly by standing in a certain relation (part of etc...) to it. Therefore, each text is potentially independent of other texts and objects as a standalone element. The designation of the relationship is conducted by grouping and assigning of the text to a corpus or an object.
