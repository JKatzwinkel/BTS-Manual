Stem Modification

Modifications of stems can be marked by writing a “!” directly behind the modified consonant. This will help the program to identify the correct lemma automatically and list it. This will just matter in the case of verbal inflections as in gmm (gmi̯).

Examples
gmm! =f (gmi̯)
wn! =f (wnn)
mꜣn! =f
