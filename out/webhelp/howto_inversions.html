Inversions

When the inverted words form a single lemma the sign-sequence is to be entered as it appears in the script.

When the inverted words do not form a single lemma each word is to be encoded with the signs that belong to it. The inverted sign-sequence cannot be reproduced.

When the inversion occurs while the word boundaries are broken , e.g. ,the signs have to be assigned to words they belong to (in this case V30+G7 and I9 separately). The inverted sign-sequence cannot be reproduced/depicted with the hieroglyphs but the program offers an option to mark such inversions.

Select Single Glyphs

This function is available for the last type of inversions. To mark the correct position of G7 determinative, select it using buttons |< < > >| . The selected sign will be marked red. After that set the "Order in Sentence" to the position where the sign occurs in the manuscript. Then do the same with the I9 sign.
