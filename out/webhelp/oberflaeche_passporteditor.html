Passport Editor

The Passport Editor section is located in the bottom part in the middle of the Text Corpus view. It contains the metadata of a selected text corpus object. It's divided into different subsections which are organized in tabs. These categories vary depending on the view: Text Corpus, Lemma, Thesaurus or Abstract Text. The rights to modify object's passport data depend on your user role for the selected corpus.

If you are working on a "child object" and want it to have the same meta-data as the "parent object", you can click "Inherit Passport Data" in the top right corner of the Passport Editor. Note that the "parent" data will be copied in the empty fields only. If the fields were filled out, their content will remain unchanged.

NOTE: the inheritance procedure applies to all tabs at once, not only the currently open one.
