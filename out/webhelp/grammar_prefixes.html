Prefixes

Only morphological prefixes are allowed to be marked by the structure sign (“:”). Do not mark any prefix of word formation.

Examples
Morphology:
correct: j:jri̯=f incorrect: jjri̯=f
Word Formation:
correct: smn incorrect: s:mn
