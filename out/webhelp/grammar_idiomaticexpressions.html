Unmarked Structure: Idiomatic Expressions

Some objects are collocated with a verb so that they form an idiomatic expression. This collocation will be determined by setting the correct entry for the verb in the lemmatizer. The object itself has to be lemmatized as it is.

Example
jri̯ ꜣh.w – to feel pain correct lemmatization: 1) jri̯ (ꜣh.w) (WCN: 851959) 2) ꜣh.w (WCN: 174)
