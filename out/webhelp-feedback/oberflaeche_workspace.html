Workspace

"Work space" is the part of the BTS user interface, located between tool bar and status bar. It changes appearance when the user switchs between "Corpus", "Lemma" or "Thesaurus" views.

The work space consists of three or four windows depending on the view you have chosen. You can maximize or minimize and relocate windows separately.
