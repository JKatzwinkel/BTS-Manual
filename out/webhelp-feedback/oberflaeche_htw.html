Hieroglyph Type Writer (HTW)

The "Hieroglyph Type Writer" is a tool which enables entering or editing hieroglyphs for the transliterated text in the "Sign-Text-Editor". "Hieroglyph Type Writer" can be found in the lower part of the BTS user interface, next to the "Passport Editor" and "Lemmatizer".

In order to use the HTW you need to open the "Sign Text Editor" and select a lemma for which hieroglyphs are to be entered.

The HTW consists of the following sections:

Input field "Type here"

Here you can enter either the Gardiner codes or "Manuel de Codage" transcription of a word chosen in the Sign-Text-Editor. In the field below the hieroglyphyc variants are offered based on your input in "Type here". The field under it displays the hieroglyphic signs the way they appear within the selected word in "Sign Text Editor".

"Select Single Glyphs"

This section offers a number of buttons to move the hierogyphic signs.

Toolbar:

Placed in the upper-right corner of the HTW, the tollbar shows buttons:

Confirm current hieroglyph editing and continue to the next word.

Remove hieroglyph data: removes already entered hieroglyphs.

Move selection to the beginning of the document.

Move selection to the beginning of the line.

Move selection to previous word.

Move selection to next word.

Move selection to end of line.

Move selection to end of document.
