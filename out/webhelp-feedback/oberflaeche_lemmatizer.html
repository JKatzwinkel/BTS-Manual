Lemmatizer

The Lemmatizer is used to reference the selected transliterated word to the lemma-entry in the lemma list. It has a number of text input fields and a toolbar.

Text input fields

Selected Word

This field displays a word that you select in the "Sign Text Editor". You can additionally change it, the list of new lemmas will be displayed in the field below. There are three sections in this field: Lemma, Flexion and Translation.

Section "Lemma" is comprised of three fields. The Lemma-list is automatically generated, based on the entry in the "Selected Word". Choosing a lemma from the list will display its "word corpus number" in the WCN field. The lemma itself is displayed in the field to the right of WCN.

Sections "Flexion" and "Translation" show the available flexions and translation options for the selected lemma. The flexion number and translation can either be selected from the available ones (lower textbox) or manually entered (upper textbox). The values in the "Translation" field depend on the Language Selection on the right.
