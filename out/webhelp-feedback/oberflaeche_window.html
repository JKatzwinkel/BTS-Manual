Switch Font Size/CSS Theme

There are two themes (blue and gray) and three font sizes available. Clicking on "Switch Font Size / CSS Theme" will consequently switch between themes and fonts sizes. If it is not yet to your liking, click again on "Switch Font Size", until you have tested all three options and made your decision. BTS will remember your personal adjustments at the startup.

Open Perspective

Window "Open perspective" comes up with entries "Lemma", "Textcorpus", "Thesaurus". Select the entry and click "OK" to switch to the relevant Workspaceof BTS (Lemma, Corpora, Thesaurus). The first time you select a different entry, it may take a while until the relevant workspace appears.

Open...

The Menu entries "Open..." open the selected windows of the current workspace in case they were minimized ( ). If the windows are already open, they will just be highlighted. Only the windows belonging to the current workspace will be opened, others will be ignored.

The following windows will be opened / highlighted depending on the workspace:

"Text corpus" workspace

"Open EgyText Editor"

"Open Lemmatizer"

"Open Passport Editor"

"Open Hieroglyph Type Writer"

"Open Annotation Part"

"Open Text Translation"

"Lemma" workspace

"Open Lemma Editor"

"Open Lemmatizer"

"Open Passport Editor"

"Open Hieroglyph Type Writer"

"Thesaurus" workspace:

"Open Annotation Part"

"Open Passport Editor"
