Changing font size

To change the font size, click "Window" in the menu bar.

Images/Menuleiste_Window.jpg

Click the first entry "Switch Font Size/CSS Theme" to change the font size.

There is no list of font sizes to choose from. The font size will be enlarged upon clicking on the entry.

Images/groesste_Schriftgroesse.jpg

There are three different sizes. After reaching the biggest size, next click will automatically bring back the smallest adjustment.
